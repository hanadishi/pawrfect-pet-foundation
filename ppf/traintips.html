TRAINING TIPS

PUPS

KITTIES

PUPS

1. Start early – Begin
training while they’re
young for faster learning.

2. Use positive
reinforcement – Reward
good behavior with
treats or praise.

3. Keep sessions short –
5–10 minutes per session
works best.

4. Be consistent – Use
the same commands and
tone every time.

5. Socialize often –
Expose them to people
and other dogs early on.

6. Stay patient – Never
yell or hit; calm repetition
builds trust.

7. End on a good note – Always finish
training with fun or a reward.

KITTIES

1. Reward with treats and
affection – Cats respond
better to kindness than
scolding.

2. Use a clicker or consistent cue – It helps them link behavior with rewards.

3. Start simple – Begin with easy tasks like “come” or using the litter box.

4. Be patient and calm – Cats learn at their own pace.

5. Redirect bad behavior – If they scratch furniture,
guide them to a scratching post instead.

6. Avoid punishment – It causes fear and stress.
